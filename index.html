Alert

An Incident that can be handled on site by the licensee through normal operating procedures and is deemed to be a very low risk to members of the public.

Communications

  • INTERNAL Notification of off-site management.
  • EXTERNAL PUBLIC ;Mandatory for individuals who have requested notification within the EPZ.
  • MEDIA Reactive as required.
  • GOVERNMENT Notify local ERCB Field Centre. Call local authority and AHS if public or media is contacted.

Actions

  • INTERNAL On site as required by licensee. Initial response undertaken in accordance with the specific or corporate level ERP.
  • EXTERNAL On site as required by licensee.

Resources

  • INTERNAL Establish what resources would be required.
  • EXTERNAL Begin to establish resources that may be required.

Level 1 Emergency

There is no danger outside the licensee's property, there is no threat to the public, and there is minimal environmental impact. The situation can be handled entirely by licensee personnel. There will be immediate control of the hazard. There is little or no media interest..

Communications

  • INTERNAL Notification of off-site management.
  • EXTERNAL PUBLIC ;Mandatory for individuals who have requested notification within the EPZ.
  • MEDIA Reactive as required.
  • GOVERNMENT Notify local ERCB Field Centre. Call local authority and AHS if public or media is contacted.

Actions

  • INTERNAL On site as required by licensee. Initial response undertaken in accordance with the specific or corporate level ERP.
  • EXTERNAL On site as required by licensee.

Resources

  • INTERNAL Establish what resources would be required.
  • EXTERNAL Begin to establish resources that may be required.

Level 2 Emergency

There is no immediate danger outside the licensee's property or the right-of-way, but there is potential for the emergency to extend beyond the licensee's property. Outside agencies must be notified. Imminent control of the hazard is probable but there is a moderate threat to the public and / or the environment. There may be local and regional media interest in the event.

Communications

  • INTERNAL Notification of off-site management.
  • EXTERNAL PUBLIC Planned and instructive in accordance with the specific ERP.
  • MEDIA Proactive media management to local or regional interest.
  • GOVERNMENT Notify local ERCB Field Centre, local authority, and AHS.

Actions

  • INTERNAL Predetermined public safety actions are under way. Corporate management team alerted and may be appropriately engaged to support on-scene responders.
  • EXTERNAL Potential for multi-agency (operator, municipal, provincial or federal) response.

Resources

  • INTERNAL Limited supplemental resources or personnel required.
  • EXTERNAL Possible assistance from government agencies and external support services required.

Level 3 Emergency

The safety of the public is in jeopardy from a major uncontrolled hazard. There are likely significant and ongoing environmental impacts. Immediate multi agency municipal and provincial government involvement is required.

Communications

  • INTERNAL Notification of off-site management.
  • EXTERNAL PUBLIC Planned and instructive in accordance with the specific ERP.
  • MEDIA Proactive media management to national interest.
  • GOVERNMENT Notify local ERCB Field Centre, local authority, and AHS.

Actions

  • INTERNAL On site as required by licensee. Initial response undertaken in accordance with the specific or corporate level ERP.
  • EXTERNAL On site as required by licensee.

Resources

  • INTERNAL Significant incremental resources required.
  • EXTERNAL Assistance from government agencies and external support services required.
