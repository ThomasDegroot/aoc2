Road Block Team Leader

The Road Block Team Leader has the responsibility to manage the movement of vehicles on access roads to and from the incident area. The Road Block Team Leader obtains briefings on the emergency situation to ensure that the road blocks are set up at safe locations. The personnel assigned to the Road Block Team Leader then isolate the defined Emergency Planning Zone using road block equipment.

The Road Block Team Leader coordinates activities with the local police and road maintenance personnel. An activity log should be maintained; recording the names of people that have passed through road blocks.
