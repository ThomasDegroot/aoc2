Operations Section Chief Roles

The Operations Section Chief will:

  • Establish the Off-Site Command Post;
  • Support Incident Commander as necessary;
  • Coordinate resources, source control and public protection measures;
  • Assign Public Protection Team Director and Source Control Team Director;
  • Assist with the necessary technology and engineering information needed to resolve the emergency situation (down-hole diagrams, facility schematics, etc);
  • Assist in the review of topographical information, weather data and weather forecast information. Compile and display incident status summary information;
  • Manage evacuation of the Emergency Planning Zone (For Alberta incidents, use the Public Protection Decision Tree in Section 8.8);
  • Review Ignition Criteria to determine if any one of the conditions have been met (this will be coordinated with Crisis Manager);
  • Take on additional responsibilities that the Incident Commander may not be able to coordinate because of the size and type of incident; and
  • Provide logistics assistance to the Source Control Team Director to address isolation, repair, spill response, and fire control as required.

Location: Corporate Emergency Operations Centre

Confers with Incident Commander

Gives Direction to:

  • Source Control Team Director; and
  • Public Protection Team Director.
