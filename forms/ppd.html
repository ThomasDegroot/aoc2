Public Protection Team Director

The Public Protection Team Director is responsible for the management of all public protection
measures. Working closely with air monitoring, evacuation and road block personnel, the Public
Protection Team Director ensures the Emergency Planning Zone is evacuated and secured in a
timely fashion.

The role of the Public Protection Team Director is to ensure that personnel patrol the emergency planning zone to locate members of the public. The Public Protection Team Director would ensure the efficient notification and / or evacuation of residents, businesses, industrial operators and transients.

The Public Protection Team Director will:

  • Proceed to the Off-Site Command Post.
  • Review the Public Protection Decision Tree
  • Record the wind direction and speed received from the Incident Commander or Operations Section Chief if assigned.
  • Remove the Emergency Response Planning Map from the Site Specific section of this document for easy reference.
  • Coordinate the following activities for the following response personnel: Road Block Team Leader, Rover Team Leader, Air Monitoring Team Leader, Reception Centre Team Leader and Telephoner Team Leader:
    • Air quality monitoring;
    • Evacuation of the public from the EPZ;
    • Contact additional resource personnel (Drilling Engineer, Facilities Engineer) as required;
    • Contact of residents to evacuate by telephone and in person;
    • Dispatches Reception Center Team designate;
    • Direct checking of emergency planning zone for transients and ensuring their evacuation, as required;
    • Set up roadblocks to isolate the EPZ.
