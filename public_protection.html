Ignition

Ignition must take place when one of the following conditions have been met:
  • Monitoring results indicate H2S concentrations in excess of 10 ppm over a 3 minute average in un-evacutated parts of the EPZ. IF MONITORING LEVELS ARE DECLINING, THEN THE SITUATION NEEDS TO BE CONTINUOSLY ASSESSED FOR IGNITION.
  • H2S concentrations in excess of 1 ppm (one hour average) detected in urban density developments.
  • Monitoring not taking place due to weather or unforeseen circumstances.
  • Release cannot be brought under control in the short term (ignition decision will be made in consultation with the ERCB).
Once any of the above conditions has been met, ignition must occur within 15 minutes of the decision to ignite.
